Dictionary App

  • Category: ICT Project
  • Institution: NGS Preah Yukunthor
  • Project date: 01 May, 2023
  • Instructor: Horm Sovanny

Place that you can learn some new words

The purpose of the ICT Project to create this dictionary is to help children under the age of 10 or 12 understand and learn words they may not have known before. This dictionary includes both English and Khmer words, along with accompanying images to enhance understanding. It aims to enable young children to learn words in their native language and in English while also associating them with visual representations. Another major goal is to promote a Khmer dictionary with English and Khmer translations combined, showcasing the work and creativity of Cambodian youth.
