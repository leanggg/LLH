Object Avioder

  • Category: Robotic Project
  • Institution: NGS Preah Yukunthor
  • Project date: 15 June, 2024
  • Instructor: TongLy Mork

Evade obstacles on its own.

This robot uses an Arduino board and ultrasonic sensors to detect and evade obstacles. It is equipped with two wheels for mobility, and the integrated code in the controller board handles its autonomous movement.
